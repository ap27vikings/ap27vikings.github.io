About our Prototype

Oasis is a safety navigation app which suggests the safest routes to help you reach your destination based on a series of key criteria such as populous streets which are well lit, CCTV-monitored, and/or frequently patrolled by authorities. Oasis provides centralized safety information for users by taking data from trusted sources and having a real-time incident reporting feature to inform other users about recent situations to be aware of in the area.
